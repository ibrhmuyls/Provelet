How to Play

Catch only the eggs that match your chosen target color.

Catching a wrong egg ends the game.

Prove slows down the fall. SP1 speeds it up!

Move your player left & right with your mouse to catch the eggs.

Click the "X" BUTTON to start the game! GOOD LUCK!
